## 🎲 Quick Rules & Card Mechanics

Jackaroo uses a standard deck of cards to move marbles around the board. Here is a quick reference table for the special card effects:

| Card | Standard Effect | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ace** | Move 1 space OR move a marble out of Base. | Essential for starting or precise finishes. |
| **Four** | Move backwards 4 spaces. | Great for dodging traps or taking shortcuts. |
| **Five** | Move any player's marble 5 spaces. | Force opponents past their safe zones. |
| **Seven** | Split 7 moves between two of your marbles. | Maximizes board presence and flexibility. |
| **Ten** | Move 10 spaces OR make an opponent skip a turn. | Powerful offensive and defensive tool. |
| **Jack** | Swap your marble with any other marble. | The ultimate comeback move. |
| **King** | Move 13 spaces OR move a marble out of Base. | Heavy movement and starting power. |
